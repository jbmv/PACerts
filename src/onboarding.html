About This Extension

This extension is designed to help Pennsylvania pharmacists review and annotate patient certificates in the PA Medical Marijuana program.

As patients are checked into MJ, and their orders are started, they will appear in the patient list of the extension. The order must be started for the data to be sent to the extension.

  • Every 30 seconds, the extension will automatically retrieve the patient DOB and State ID necessary to view their certificate.
  • Once the patient data necessary for viewing the certificate, the status will change to "View Certificate".
  • Clicking this will open the DOH page and summarize their certificate for review. This extension modifies the DOH page to jump to the annotation box and summarizes the information in the certificate. It also auto-reloads the page once the saved button is clicked.
  • Once your dispensary note is saved, the patient is automatically hidden from the list unless "Show Completed" is checked.

Warning!

This extension has permission to intercept, store, modify, and submit data on your behalf.

The code is publicly available for inspection on github.

This extension injects code into mjplatform.com and padohmmp.custhelp.com websites that has the ability to:

  • Intercept any data transmitted to or from these sites including any username/password/session token/cookie as well as protected health information
  • Modify, store, and transmit data on your behalf to the above websites.

Currently, local stored data, including patient data, is not stored encrypted. Please ensure your organization is aware of this limitation and that it complies with internal policies.

Getting Started

Most of the actions you will take with this extension happen from the popup window accessed by clicking on the extension icon. Pin the extension to the chrome extenion bar for easy access. In order to activate the extension, you must log in to MJ using the link from the extension popup window. This prevents other workers in your facility from accidentally activating the extension.

Usage Guide

Header Section:
  • This section shows the Facility Name and the App Status.
  • The status buttons for each page will turn green when the extension is receiving data from the respective pages.
  • If the status button turns red, this indicates a loss of communication to the extension. Please re-open or re-load the respective page by clicking on the button.
Patient Section:
  • This section is auto-populated when a patient is checked into the store and their order is started.
  • The patient cert notes are populated once the patient is looked up in the DOH portal
    • "Limitation" -- the patient has a limitation set by the certifying physician
    • "First Visit" -- this is the patient's first visit on their first certificate ever
    • "Indications" -- lists the indications on the certificate
  • The patient status reflects the next step to take to finish reviewing the certificate:
    • "Search MJ" -- clicking this will lookup the patient data necessary to view the certificate
    • "View Certificate" -- This will load the DOH page for this patient, summarize the certificate, and display that info above the DOH comment box
    • "Already Certed Today" -- the patient has a pharmacist comment
    • "Completed" -- The patient certificate has been reviewed. The patient is automatically hidden after review is complete.
      Check the "Show Completed" chckbox to show these patients.
Footer Section:
  • This section contains a summary of the data as well as the extension options.
  • The count at the bottom reflects the number of patients shown. Filtered counts are from the hidden, completed patients.
  • When a patient record is selected a Mark Completed button will appear. This button will manually mark a patient as completed. This should be used if the certificate was reviewed outside of the app.
  • The bottom right section contains two options radio buttons.
    • Sounds: Turns notification sounds on or off. The extension will play a sound whenever a new patient checks in and has their order started. It will play a warning sound if one of the MJ or DOH pages is disconnected. In auto-cert mode, patient sounds are only played if there is an issue with the certificate that needs manual review.
    • Auto Cert: This mode automatically annotates the certificate if the patient is not a first visit, has no limitations, and has a valid indication. YOU MUST OBTAIN APPROVAL FROM YOUR ORGANIZATION AND THE PA DOH TO USE THIS FEATURE.
